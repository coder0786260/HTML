DID YOU KNOW?

welcome to the coolest facts page on the internet

FACT:1

Sound travels four times faster in water than in air. Because water is denser than air, sound waves can travel more efficiently through it.

FACT:2

Octopuses have three hearts. Two hearts pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps blood to the rest of the body.

FACT:3

Bananas are berries, but strawberries are not. Botanically speaking, bananas meet the criteria for being classified as berries, while strawberries do not.

FACT:4

Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible.

FACT:5

Sharks existed before trees. Sharks have been around for more than 400 million years, while the first trees appeared around 350 million years ago.

FACT:6

Shakespeare invented more than 1,700 words. By changing nouns into verbs, connecting words, and adding prefixes/suffixes, he introduced everyday words like lonely, gossip, elbow (as a verb), and uncomfortable to the English language.
